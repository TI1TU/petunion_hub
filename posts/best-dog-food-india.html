Best Dog Food in India (Healthy & Budget Options)

Mar 31, 2026 By PetUnion Hub

Introduction

Choosing the right food for your dog is essential for their health. With so many options available in India, it can be incredibly confusing to decide exactly what’s best for your furry friend. Let's break down the essential types and brands.

Types of Dog Food 🥩

You have four primary paths when deciding what to feed your dog:

  • Dry food (kibble): The most common, convenient, and great for dental health.
  • Wet food: Phenomenal for picky eaters and hydration.
  • Homemade food: Time-consuming but you have ultimate control over the ingredients.
  • Raw diet: Highly debated, but favored by many for its evolutionary appropriateness.

Popular Dog Food Brands in India 🇮🇳

When walking into an Indian pet store, these are the most highly-rated choices you will encounter:

  • Royal Canin: Premium diets segmented heavily by breed.
  • Pedigree: The most recognizable and widely available budget option.
  • Drools: An affordable brand offering real chicken combos.
  • Farmina: A premium, biologically appropriate brand gaining massive popularity in India.

Homemade vs. Packaged Food 🍲

Homemade Food:
✔ Very fresh and completely natural.
❌ Extremely time-consuming to cook and balance daily.

Packaged Food:
✔ Unbeatable convenience and precisely balanced nutrients.
❌ May contain unwanted preservatives or fillers.

Feeding Tips 🐾

  • Feed strictly according to age and weight.
  • Avoid overfeeding to prevent joint issues later in life.
  • Keep a fixed, reliable feeding schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ❓

Q: Can I give milk to dogs?
Some dogs are highly lactose intolerant — it is generally advised to avoid it entirely, or only give in exceedingly small amounts.

Conclusion

A balanced diet ensures your dog stays incredibly healthy, active, and happy throughout their entire lifespan. Always choose high-quality food based entirely on your dog’s specific needs. If you are ever unsure, consult your local veterinarian!
